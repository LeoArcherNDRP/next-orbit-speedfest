Midweek Madness

And boy was it chaotic..

Midweek Madness fulfilled that name, and then some. What was initially a tester for the day of the week, and time of day to try and allow people that usually cannot attend the later events to attend. Renting out some awesome hotring trucks, getting the casino track paved over, we wanted to give people something fun to race.
