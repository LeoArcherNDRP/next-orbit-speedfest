Midweek Madness

And boy was it chaotic..

Midweek Madness lived up to its name — and then some. What was meant to be a simple weekday test event turned into one of the most chaotic showdowns we’ve hosted yet.

Renting out a batch of hotring trucks and paving over the casino track gave racers something totally different to handle — and judging by the crashes, the spins, and the screams over the radio? They definitely felt the difference.

Despite the madness, the turnout was incredible. Drivers who normally can't make the late-week events finally had their shot, and they brought the energy with them.

In classic NOS fashion — it was messy, chaotic, loud, and absolutely unforgettable.
