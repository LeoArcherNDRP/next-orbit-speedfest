About Us

We’re here to unleash the thrill of racing for everyone in this state, offering an experience that is safe, legal, and completely worry free. Our mission is to bring people together through the excitement of true competition while giving them a place where they can push their limits with total confidence. This is the racing experience you have been waiting for, and we are proud to make it a reality for our entire community.

Meet the Team!

Our team is a community of people bound by a shared passion for NOS. Some chase the thrill of the track, others stand behind them with unwavering support, and every one of them is essential. Together, they bring heart, strength, and purpose to this business, and it is their collective passion that fuels our success.
